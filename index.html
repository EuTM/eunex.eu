Speakers:

Axel Friese (CO:FORWARD, former TU München)

Axel co-authored the definitive book ‘Business Model Generation’. He is an experienced entrepreneur, worked at Roland Berger and Evonil. Axel has taught enterpreneurship at one Europe’s leading univesities the TU München. He founded the CO:FORWARD incubator in Herzogenrath/Aachen. He studied at UniBw Munich, has an M.B.A. from European University (Brussels) / University of Texas at Austin (UT Austin).

Vidar Andersen (startup founder, corporate innovation)

Vidar is well-known in the European Startup world. Vidar is a certified Launchpad Instructor by Steve Blank. He spoke at several conferences like the Websummit and a couple of his startups were covered by global media like the New York Times. Today, he helps startups and corporates to grow their business faster. He also teaches at universities like the University of Cologne.


Rene Maurer (RWTH, well-known German expert on Effectuation)

Rene is Germany’s leading expert on effectuation. He’s the man behind www.effectuation.de. He works at the RWTH Aachen on Innovation and Entrepreneurship. He worked at 3M, several startups and has written several books on successful entrepreneurs.


Omar Mohout (Sirris)

Omar is all about go-to-market, growth and scaling of technology companies. He’s well-known in Belgium and the rest of the world as Growthhacker. He has written a book called: “Lean Pricing”. Omar is a serial entrepreneur and has founded several startups. Today he is an advisor and mentor to startups. We’re very lucky to this rising star and hand-on lecturer on board. .
