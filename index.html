Web, Wurst, Windmills and Waffles (WeWuWiWa) is now organizing the best entrepreneurial program you can get in the Euregio. Never before in the Euregio were you able to attend such an exclusive and high-end course for entepreneurs. You’ll be taught on the most hot topics in entrepreneurs by the best trainers, entrepreneurs and coaches from Germany, The Netherlands and Belgium.

In the Euregio Entrepreneurial Experience you’ll learn about the Lean Startup, Business Model Canvas and Growth hacking.

You also get to know the community and work with a diverse range of entrepreneurs in this program. We work based on entrepreneurial experiences and scientific analytics tools.
